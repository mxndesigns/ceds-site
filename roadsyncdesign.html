RoadSync
Design

The Goal

To design a simple, intuitive, and accessible product for RoadSync's users, many of whom are not tech-savvy and may be unfamiliar with digital payment solutions.

Timeframe

1 year

Platforms / Frameworks

Web App, Responsive, Design System, Flutter

Services

UI/UX, Design System,
User Research,
User Flows, UX Operations
Visual Design

How did we Decrease Time for Task Completion across the product?


What was the Challenge?

Many of RoadSync's users, particularly brokers and carriers, are often small, family-run businesses that are hesitant to adopt new technologies. Our UX team focused on showcasing the tangible benefits of RoadSync by simplifying payment processes, streamlining expense management, and offering user-friendly features that improve efficiency and create a seamless experience for both businesses and their drivers.

The Solution

After confirming the need of users during monthly customer call shadows and interviews we determined the best approach would be to provide a easy way for drivers, carriers, brokers, and warehouses to receive their funds faster overall. ensuring a seamless and efficient parking process.


Key Initiatives

  • Decreased the time it takes for payments to settle.
  • Allowed drivers to easily submit expense reports.
  • Provided seamless payment processing options.
  • Allowed fast-onboarding of brokers & carriers to manage payments.
  • Offered Instant Payouts for Drivers on payday.

Final Results, Outcomes, and Learnings


While the work at Roadsync was challenging we were able to deliver a number of key deliverables that helped the company move forward in the right direction. The amount of detail, research, and effort that went into the project was a great learning experience and a testament to the power of a small but MIGHTY team of scrappy individuals.

Our Key Results / Outcomes

  • Decreased user confusion for existing driver's.
  • Captured funds and settle payments faster.
  • Added more shared UX patterns for major actions across the product.
  • Increased partnerships due to more self-service options.
  • Better organized reporting data for payment tracking.

Relic
App

The Goal

Relic aims to successfully connect fans to celebrities in a safe environment for things such as events, book releases, product launches, and more.

Timeframe

1 year

Platforms / Frameworks

Web App, Responsive, Design System, Flutter

Services

Startup, User Research, User Flows, Content Design, Design Strategy

What's the
Problem?

As the founding designer of Relic, I was tasked with thinking of the core UX for the first version of Relic. The communication between fans and celebrities is currently disjointed due to trust, safety, and limited access. Relic aims to successfully connect fans to celebrities in a safe environment for things such as events, book releases, product launches, and more.

    Primary product goals for first launch
  • Provide a secure & safe space for controlled events with fans.
  • Allow fans to receive crypto artifiacts that can gain value over time.
  • Introduce an early on scaleable solution for the product.

Agree on North Star objectives, key results, and initiatives to achieve them.



The Solution

After chatting with multiple celebs that have voiced their concern with a more controlled environment with their fans, the ultimate solution was to provide a space for fans to receive a signature from their favorite celebs.
